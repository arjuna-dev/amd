Our philosophy


 Can be summarized as"Happiness for oneself and service to humanity".

    read more...

 We all are in the search for true, ever-lasting happiness but we fail to attain it because we look for it in the wrong places. It is in meditation where we can find our ever-free nature, inside us. And it is through service that we can give meaning to our lives and the world that surrounds us. These two go together, one is not possible without the other.

Dharma

"Just as all-round physical exercise makes the body fit, similarly appropriate psychic and spiritual exercise leads to one’s psychic evolution and spiritual elevation. If one is keen to advance, if one wants to attain expansion as well as bliss in life, one must continue to struggle. Spiritual practice is an all-round struggle leading to all-round progress and the ultimate goal of merging in the Supreme Source."
